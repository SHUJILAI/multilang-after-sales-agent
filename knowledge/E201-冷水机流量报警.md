## 症状 Symptom
冷却水流量低于安全阈值，激光器被禁止出光。
Cooling water flow is below the safety threshold; laser emission is locked.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 检查水箱水位，低于刻度线则加纯净水至标线。Check the tank level; top up with purified water to the mark if low.
2. 检查水管有无折弯、压扁，接头是否漏水。Check hoses for kinks or crushing, and joints for leaks.
3. 拆洗或更换滤芯（建议每 3 个月一次）。Clean or replace the filter cartridge (recommended every 3 months).
4. 听水泵有无异响；异响或不出水请停机报修。Listen for abnormal pump noise; if noisy or no water output, stop and request service.
5. 故障排除后报警自动复位；未复位则重启冷水机。The alarm resets automatically once fixed; otherwise restart the chiller.
