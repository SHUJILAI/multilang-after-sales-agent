## 症状 Symptom
按出光键无激光，或实测功率明显偏低。
No beam when firing, or measured power is significantly low.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 确认急停已释放，激光器钥匙开关在 ON 位。Make sure the E-stop is released and the laser key switch is ON.
2. 检查出光使能联锁：门板、光闸是否全部闭合。Check emission interlocks: doors and shutter fully closed.
3. 检查保护镜片有无烧点或污染（见 E302），有则更换。Inspect the protective lens for burn spots or contamination (see E302); replace if needed.
4. 在控制面板核对功率、频率、占空比参数是否被误改。Verify power, frequency and duty-cycle parameters on the panel.
5. 以上正常仍无出光：多为光路偏移或激光器故障。If all above is normal: likely beam-path misalignment or laser source fault.

## 注意 Warning
禁止自行拆修光路，请联系工程师远程诊断。Do NOT disassemble the beam path yourself. Contact a service engineer for remote diagnosis.
