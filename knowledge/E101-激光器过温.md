## 症状 Symptom
激光器内部温度超过 45°C，系统自动停机保护。
Internal laser temperature exceeded 45°C; the system shuts down for protection.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 立即停机，保持冷水机继续运行帮助散热。Stop the machine and keep the chiller running to dissipate heat.
2. 检查冷水机水温是否高于 35°C，偏高则等待降温。Check chiller water temperature; if above 35°C, wait for it to cool down.
3. 检查激光器进风口滤网，积灰用压缩空气吹扫。Inspect the air-intake filter; blow off dust with compressed air.
4. 确认水流量正常（流量开关指示灯亮）。Confirm normal water flow (flow-switch indicator on).
5. 水温降至 30°C 以下后按复位键重启激光器。When water drops below 30°C, press RESET to restart the laser.

## 注意 Warning
连续两次报警请停机并联系工程师，禁止强行重启。If the alarm occurs twice in a row, stop and contact a service engineer. Do NOT force a restart.
