## 症状 Symptom
切割辅助气体压力低于工艺要求，切割质量下降或系统停机。
Assist gas pressure is below the process requirement; cut quality degrades or the system stops.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 检查空压机是否运行，储气罐压力是否 ≥0.8 MPa。Check the compressor is running and tank pressure is ≥0.8 MPa.
2. 沿管路用肥皂水检查接头漏气。Check fittings along the line for leaks (soap-water test).
3. 按工艺表核对减压阀设定：碳钢氧气约 0.8-1.5 bar；不锈钢氮气 12-20 bar。Verify regulator settings per the process table: ~0.8-1.5 bar O2 for mild steel; 12-20 bar N2 for stainless.
4. 检查气路过滤器积水，及时排水。Drain any water accumulated in the air filter.
5. 压力仍不足：核对空压机排量是否匹配，或联系工程师。Still low: check compressor capacity, or contact an engineer.
