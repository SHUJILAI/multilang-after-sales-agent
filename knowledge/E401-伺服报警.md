## 症状 Symptom
驱动器面板报警，或开机后轴无法完成回零。
Drive alarm on the panel, or the axis fails to home after power-on.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 关机 30 秒后重启，重新执行回零。Power off for 30 seconds, restart and home again.
2. 检查导轨、齿条有无异物卡滞，清理后手动推轴确认顺畅。Check rails and racks for debris; clean and push the axis by hand to confirm smooth motion.
3. 检查限位开关是否被铁屑遮挡或损坏。Check whether limit switches are blocked by chips or damaged.
4. 记录驱动器面板上的报警代码并拍照。Record and photograph the drive alarm code.
5. 重启后仍报警：将报警代码发给工程师远程诊断。Still alarming after restart: send the code to a service engineer for remote diagnosis.

## 注意 Warning
勿自行拆驱动器或电机，存在触电与二次损坏风险。Do NOT open drives or motors yourself — electric shock and secondary damage risk.
