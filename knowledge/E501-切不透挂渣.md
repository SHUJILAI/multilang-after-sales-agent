## 症状 Symptom
板材切不透，断面挂渣多、粗糙。
Material not cut through; rough edge with heavy dross.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 先查保护镜片是否污染（见 E302）——功率衰减最常见原因。First check the protective lens (see E302) — contamination is the most common cause of power loss.
2. 核对焦点位置：碳钢一般负焦，不锈钢正焦。Verify focus position: usually negative for mild steel, positive for stainless.
3. 核对切割速度与功率是否匹配板厚（参照随机工艺表）。Match speed and power to thickness (see the process table).
4. 检查喷嘴磨损、口径与中心度。Check nozzle wear, orifice size and centering.
5. 检查气体纯度与气压（氮气纯度建议 ≥99.95%）。Check gas purity and pressure (N2 purity ≥99.95% recommended).
6. 以上正常仍切不透：激光器功率可能衰减，联系工程师实测功率。Still failing: laser power may have degraded — contact an engineer for measurement.
