## 症状 Symptom
切割头保护镜片脏污或烧蚀，导致功率下降、切不透。
Dirt or burn spots on the protective window cause power loss and incomplete cutting.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 停机断电，待切割头冷却后拆下镜座。Stop and power off; remove the lens holder after the head cools down.
2. 用无水乙醇加无尘棉签单方向轻擦，禁止来回擦。Wipe gently in ONE direction with absolute ethanol and lint-free swabs — never back and forth.
3. 有烧点或裂纹必须更换新镜片，禁止继续使用。Replace the lens if there are burn spots or cracks. Never keep using it.
4. 装回后重新做喷嘴同轴校准（打胶带法）。Re-check nozzle centering after reassembly (tape-shot method).
5. 建议每班检查一次；切割碳钢时每 2 小时检查一次。Inspect every shift; every 2 hours when cutting mild steel.
