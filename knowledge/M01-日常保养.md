## 说明 Note
按周期执行保养可显著降低故障率。以下为厂家推荐周期。
Following the schedule significantly reduces failure rates.

## 保养周期 Schedule
1. 每班：吹扫镜片、清理台面熔渣、倒废料车。Every shift: blow off lens dust, clean slag from the table, empty the scrap cart.
2. 每周：导轨、齿条、丝杠加润滑脂；清理电柜滤网。Weekly: grease rails, racks and screws; clean the cabinet filter.
3. 每月：更换冷水机纯净水并清洗水箱；检查气管老化。Monthly: replace chiller water and clean the tank; check air hoses for aging.
4. 每季：断电后清理激光器与电柜内部灰尘，紧固接线端子。Quarterly: power off, clean dust inside the laser and cabinet, tighten terminals.
5. 每年：由工程师做光路全面校准与功率检测。Yearly: full beam-path calibration and power measurement by an engineer.
