## 症状 Symptom
冷水机出水温度超过设定上限，散热效率下降。
Chiller outlet water exceeds the set limit; cooling efficiency drops.

## 处理步骤 Steps
1. 确认环境温度不超过 40°C，设备四周留出散热空间。Ensure ambient temperature is below 40°C with clearance around the unit.
2. 将设定温度调至 26-28°C（夏季建议 28°C 防结露）。Set target temperature to 26-28°C (28°C in summer to avoid condensation).
3. 断电后清洗冷凝器翅片上的灰尘油污。Power off and clean dust/oil from the condenser fins.
4. 水温持续高于 32°C 多为冷媒泄漏导致制冷量不足，请报修。If water stays above 32°C, refrigerant leak is likely — request service.
